About the Memorial

Inspired by the testimonials of Walterina Markova, a distinct case of sexual slavery by the Imperial Japanese. The Markova Archives serves to collect, preserve, and document all relevant memories of all sexual wartime survivors for public dissemination and future generations.

Spearheaded by Michael Nelmida, who previously served at the National Archives of the Philippines, he is currently finishing his Masters in Human Rights and Democratization at Universitas Gadjah Mada and lectures at University of the Philippines-Mindanao.
